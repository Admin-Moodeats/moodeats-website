About MoodEats Inc.

MoodEats Inc. is a Canadian technology company building AI-powered food, grocery, and discovery experiences.

Our goal is to make food decisions feel more natural, more personal, and more intelligent. MoodEats is being developed as a platform that helps users move from feeling and intent to relevant meals, grocery baskets, and discovery experiences with less friction.

What we are building

MoodEats is focused on combining conversational AI, personalization, context, and operational ordering flows into one product experience. The platform direction includes restaurant ordering, grocery basket building, personalized food discovery, and intelligent recommendation systems.

Company status

MoodEats is currently in production and pre-launch development. Public launch timing, platform availability, and distribution updates will be announced through official MoodEats channels.
