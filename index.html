How it works

Three steps.
Zero thinking.

Tell Rosa how you feel — or just take a selfie. She handles the rest.

🧠
01
Share your mood

Type how you feel, speak it out loud, or take a quick selfie. Rosa — our AI — reads your mood in seconds using text, voice analysis, or facial detection.

✨
02
Get perfect suggestions

Rosa factors in your mood, the weather, time of day, your order history, and your location to suggest exactly the right food — not just a menu dump.

🚀
03
Order in one tap

Confirm your order, choose delivery or pickup, and you're done. MoodEats handles restaurant matching, grocery sourcing, and real-time tracking.

Selfie Mood Detection

Take a photo and Rosa reads your facial expression to detect your emotional state — stress, happiness, tiredness — and tailors food suggestions to match what your body actually needs.

🎙️
Voice Ordering

Say what you want. Rosa listens, understands context, and builds your order. No tapping through menus. Just talk to her like a person.

Hunger Prediction

MoodEats learns when you usually eat. It sends smart nudges right before your typical meal times — not random notifications, but perfectly-timed suggestions based on your actual habits.

Real-time Tracking

Live driver location, ETA updates, and order status — all inside the app. From "order placed" to "at your door," you're never left wondering.
