Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

MoodEats Inc. (“MoodEats,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we may collect, use, store, and protect information when you visit our website, communicate with us, or use the MoodEats platform and related services.

1. Information We May Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, and contact details you provide to us.
  • Account, support, or inquiry information submitted through our website or services.
  • Device, browser, and usage information such as IP address, pages viewed, and interaction data.
  • Order, preference, and food-related information when using the MoodEats platform.
  • Location information where required to support ordering, delivery, or nearby recommendations.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate, improve, and maintain our website and platform.
  • To respond to inquiries, support requests, and business communications.
  • To personalize user experiences, including food and grocery recommendations.
  • To improve safety, security, analytics, and platform performance.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to understand website traffic, improve functionality, and support performance and security. More details are available in our Cookie Policy.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information with service providers, hosting providers, analytics providers, payment partners, communications providers, and operational partners where reasonably necessary to run our business and services. We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect our rights and users.

5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for business, operational, legal, security, and record-keeping purposes.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of certain personal information, subject to applicable legal and operational limitations.

8. Children

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age required by applicable law, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at privacy@moodeats.ca or admin@moodeats.ca.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
