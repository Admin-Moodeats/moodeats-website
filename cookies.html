Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how MoodEats Inc. uses cookies and similar technologies on moodeats.ca and related digital properties.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function, remember preferences, measure traffic, and improve performance.

2. How We May Use Cookies

  • To help our website function properly.
  • To understand how visitors use our website.
  • To improve performance, usability, and reliability.
  • To support security and fraud prevention.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential cookies: required for core website functionality.
  • Performance cookies: help us understand site usage and improve performance.
  • Preference cookies: remember user choices where applicable.

4. Third-Party Tools

We may use third-party services such as hosting, analytics, or infrastructure providers that set or access cookies or similar technologies as part of delivering services.

5. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. You may block or delete cookies, although doing so may affect how parts of the website function.

6. Contact

For questions about this Cookie Policy, contact privacy@moodeats.ca or admin@moodeats.ca.
